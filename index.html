About us

In our state of the art production facility we do produce our own brand Sappy as well as licensed products for international markets. The quality and taste of our products are the two most important things that we care mostly since a happy consumer is our mission. In addition to our highest quality production technology, flexibility, fast response to market demands and extraordinary customer service are the strengths that makes us evolve, grow and develop new products with a wide range of formats and flavors. Our goal is to develop products that nobody will say NO. Products that will be approved by parents and loved by kids. Products without preservatives, artificial colors and flavors. All our stakeholders we do see them as a real partner for whom we do have a lot of respect and we do strongly believe that only by respecting them, listening their needs and requirements and combining our ideas and strengths we can create added value for our consumers and benefits for our partners. We warmly invite you to discover our product range.
